Love | Serve | Inspire

Namastey,


The Co-founder and director of Preseed Ventures Pvt. Ltd. Nishchal Kesarwani. He is on a journey to attempt building beautiful web products useful to millions someday, including you; and on that journey he also writes.

He builds at preseed.in and writes on medium.

His life's purpose may be summed up as following :-

1. Karma - His purpose is to work in the area of liberating people through technology entrepreneurship of the righteous intent, so that at least some of them could truly and soulfully do that what is meaningful to our society.

2. Dharma - He walks the path of karma also to help (wo)mankind make progress through their own actions towards liberating them from their non scientific and rigid beliefs of life and universe, so that they could do what they find harmony in. Hence, his purpose is also to ensure that the soul is not ignored any more. His purpose is to understand and impart the awareness of the god's vibe through yoga and meditation.

3. Seva - His purpose is to ensure that each life he touches on this journey to building products gets truly served in their righteous progress. That is how we will realise the value of our products, not through the value of money. He don't thinks his creations are quite there yet, but he do believe that if he persevere enough, his creations will be there, for he is eternally committed to them.

The things he does in a day, enables him to stumble upon many teams with a good idea and a strong will to build further upon their ideas, with thier skills, perhaps even a sound prototype, logistical and back end capabilities. Almost invariably, if he find them good enough to ping his mind, he would have views for them that could help them make further progress. But these views are not mere theoretical views, these are strategies that need to be executed beyond the current capabilities of the prototype team. So, in order to help these teams at different stages of the startup life cycle we have allowed for such teams to engage with Nishchal directly. Although this will be useful when startup teams are willing to stay focused on the strategy proposed by Nishchal above all else. As he progress on this journey of building and writing, he will sum up my experiences in a book about simplicity, curiosity and entrepreneurship that may have the potential to inspire a whole generation.
